Adds value-conditional `<Requires>` props (`eq`, `neq`, `gt`, `gte`, `lt`, `lte`, `in`, `notIn`, `truthy`, `falsy`) and `when` escape hatch for raw DSL expressions, powered by `@umpire/dsl`. Adds `<FairWhen>` for field-level value validation, top-level `<StandaloneDisables>` and `<OneOf>` components, and `reason` prop support on all rule components.
